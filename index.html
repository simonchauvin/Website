/** About me **/

I’m a PhD candidate at the Conservatoire National des Arts et Métiers (CNAM) in Paris. After studying computer science and game design, I’m now using all this knowledge to unveil the specific nature of narratives in games. I also develop games on my spare time as a game designer and programmer and I particularly enjoy games with a strong focus on simple mechanics, proceduralism and/or expressiveness, but I’m still undecided about which one I prefer.
